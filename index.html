About Me



My name is Diamond Williams. I am a aspiring Software Engineer and World changer. I am currently enrolled in University of Pennsylvania's Full Stack Development BootCamp and planning to continue pursuing my Bachelors of Science in Software Engineering.

My hope is to inspire young black and brown girls to explore the world of Tech, in hopes that they can see a world of possibilities for themselves and others with computer technology.

I am confident with the skills I am learning an will continue to learn order I can become a integral part of the growth and success of an amazing company. I am a driven, self-motivated, problem solver with the desire to change the world through technology.
